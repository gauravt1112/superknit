LOCATION & INFRASTRUCTURE
Our factory building in Daman is an independent RCC industrial structure, established using superlative techniques of construction. The 3 floors of the factory building are designed to provide a clean and pristine production environment and utmost convenience in order to carry out each step efficiently in the production process. Our entire sock manufacturing process is carried out in this building.
SKI Office
Our office is located in Lower Parel (W), in Mumbai, India. We are 8 kilometers from the city center and are also well connected by the rail system. The closest station is Lower Parel Station, Western Railways, which is a mere 5 minutes away from our office.
SKI Factory
Our manufacturing unit is located in Daman, India. Our factory is 190 kilometers from Mumbai, and is on the Mumbai-Delhi National Highway no. 8. The nearest station is Vapi Railway Station that is 8 kilometers from our factory. Our factory is very accessible by rail and road.
