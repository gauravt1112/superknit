ABOUT SKI
Established on 18th May 1999, Super Knit Industries
is a proprietorship that is one of India's largest manufacturers of socks.
Our knitting machines range from 96 needles all the way up to 240 needles and we have both single and double cylinder machines, due to this we are able to supply a variety of socks to our buyers. We specialize in 200 needles fine gauge 100% gassed mercerized cotton socks, as required in the high quality conscious markets of Europe.
Our philosophy is to build a long lasting
relationship with our prospective clients.
Our main objective at Super Knit Industries is to ensure that our clients are satisfied with the end product. We inspect our socks at various stages of manufacturing, to ensure they receive premium quality products.
