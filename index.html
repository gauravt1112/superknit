COMPANY INSIGHT
Super Knit Industries is one of the largest manufactures of socks in India, with a very wide range of machinery, which allows us to manufacture practically any sock that our customer may require.
Our proprietor, Mr. Naval Saraf, has a history of over 3 decades in the sock manufacturing industry and his obsession since inception has been Quality! Quality! Quality!
With the help of his multiple years of experience and knowledge, we can proudly say we make socks that surpass our buyer's expectation.
OUR CLIENTS
Our philosophy is to sustainably grow our business along with our clients and suppliers. We believe all parties must seek mutual benefit from the conducted business, thereby ensuring a long lasting association.
INHOUSE BRANDS
In 2012, we launched our brand in India by the name Supersox. Since inception we have been in Exports, but we saw a gap in the Indian market, and we saw there was a lack of quality socks being sold in India. So we have taken the same technology we have been using for our export buyers and we are now providing the same high quality and wide range to our Indian customers.
